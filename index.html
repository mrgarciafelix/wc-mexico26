How "build from your wins" actually works

Each stake is a slice of your current bankroll (Kelly). Win and tonight's result makes tomorrow's slice bigger; lose and it shrinks — automatic compounding that can't bust you. Unlike a roulette system, this only grows money because the bets are positive-EV: the model's probability beats the bookmaker's price. The singles compound fastest; the "let it ride" parlay trades growth for a shot at a big day. Bet today's card → it settles tonight → update your bankroll above → repeat.
